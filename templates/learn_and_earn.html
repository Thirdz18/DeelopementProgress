1 What Changed — Old vs New

GoodMarket Learn & Earn has undergone a major update. Instead of receiving instant G$ rewards, you now earn an Achievement NFT that can be sold or burned to get G$ tokens.

Take Quiz  →  Achievement Card  →  Mint as NFT  →  Sell or Burn for G$
❌ Before (Old) ✅ Now (New)
After Quiz Instant G$ reward Achievement Card only
Card Action "Sell This Card" button Mint as NFT on Celo
NFT No NFT Full NFT on Celo blockchain
Earning G$ Sent directly & immediately Sell or burn your NFT
Eligibility All quizzes eligible Feb 11, 2026 onwards only
2 How to Take a Quiz
1Connect your GoodDollar wallet by clicking "Connect GoodDollar Wallet"
2Navigate to the Learn & Earn page
3Click the "Start Quiz" button
4Answer each question — you have 20 seconds per question
5Submit your answers — your Achievement Card will appear
⚠️ Important: You must connect your wallet first. Only one quiz every 120 hours (5 days) is allowed per wallet.
3 Achievement Card & NFT Minting

After completing a quiz, your Achievement Card appears automatically showing your score and available actions.

1Click the "🎨 Mint as NFT" button on your Achievement Card
2Wait for minting — the app pays the gas fee for you
3You will see "✅ NFT #[number] Minted!" confirmation
4Your NFT appears in the "My NFTs" tab
⛽ Gas fees are FREE — GoodMarket covers all minting costs on your behalf.

💡 The Feb 11, 2026 cutoff prevents mass minting from old cards and ensures marketplace fairness.
5 NFT Marketplace — Sell & Buy
🏷️ For Sellers
1. Go to My NFTs tab 2. Click "List for Sale" 3. Confirm the price 4. NFT appears in marketplace 5. Receive G$ when sold ✅
💰 For Buyers
1. Go to NFT Marketplace tab 2. Click "Buy NFT" 3. Approve G$ in wallet 4. NFT is yours ✅
💡 Price formula: (score ÷ total questions) × 1,000 G$ — you can delist anytime before sale.
6 How to Burn an NFT

Burning permanently destroys an NFT in exchange for a G$ reward based on your quiz score.

🔥 Burn Reward Formula
(score ÷ total questions) × 1,000 G$
10/10 = 1,000 G$ 9/10 = 900 G$ 8/10 = 800 G$ 7/10 = 700 G$ 5/10 = 500 G$
⚠️ Burning is permanent — the NFT cannot be recovered. Delist from marketplace first before burning.
7 Transaction History

The "📜 Tx History" tab shows all your NFT transactions — sales and burns — with verifiable CeloScan links.

Section Contents
💰 NFT Sales Token #, Quiz, Price, Seller, Buyer, TX hash, Date
🔥 Burn History Token #, Quiz, Score, G$ Reward, Burn TX, Date
